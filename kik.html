Summer Internship, 2015

During the summer of 2015 I interned at Kik and worked on several impactful projects. My team worked on content related features, and during my internship I worked on projects for sharing and finding content, and even designed some Kik swag.

iOS App Extension

I designed the Kik iOS app extension. Designed to be easy to use and focus on the content being shared. Greatly removed friction for users to share content on the apps. Made animations and interactions with Pixate. Read More

GIFs on Kik

I designed a GIF sharing and browsing experience to allow for easy expression of emotion. Animations and interactions were made in Pixate.

Kik Share Logo

I designed another logo for Kik. Their main one with the name "kik" also being their logo doesn't work in all contexts while this one is more versatile and communicates the brand effectively.
