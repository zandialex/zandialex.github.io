About Me

I'm a designer who studies CS at the University of Waterloo. In the past I've worked at cool companies like Kik and Yelp where I've been an engineer and a designer, although I am a designer at heart. I am an interdisciplinary product designer with a drive for understanding people and relationships. I also love creating prototypes with Framer.

I've always found myself at the intersection of technology and design because I believe that technical innovation is not enough, it is the harmony of the two that makes something truly special.
