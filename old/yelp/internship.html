Design + Engineering Internships

I was lucky enough to intern at Yelp as a designer and engineer. As an engineer I updated the mobile site to a more modern UI and made it use reusable style guide components. As a designer I worked on many impactful projects relating to growing the Business Messaging feature and Business Money projects.

Lessons Learned

Understand How the Product is Built

Having intered on the front-end team working on reusable UI components, I realized how important it is to understand the underlying structure of a software product. This improves the design process by making the constraints much more clear, and greatly improves collaboration with engineers.

Balance Business and Design Goals

Keeping a delicate balance between business and design goals requires making good trade-offs. This comes from an understanding of how the business makes money and effective inter-team collaboartion.

Articulate Design Decisions

Having worked with several teams across different disciplines it became necessary to describe design in a way each person would understand. This is critical for collaboration and a well made product.

The Mobile Site

I rewrote lots of the Yelp mobile site front-end to use reusable UI components from the Yelp Styleguide. This keeps the UI up-to-date and consistent with other Yelp platforms.
