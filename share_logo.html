Share Logo

Kik Messenger

Problem

Kik had just one logo that didn't work in many cases (e.g small space like an Apple Watch icon). Lots of our partners that provided us content were displaying buttons on their app to "share to Kik" but it was often presented in a very unflattering way. There wasn't very good documentation for our content providers and partners to use our logo, and the one that they used was not designed to work in small spaces.


Current logo

A typical partner share screen (small Kik logo)

Goals

  • Make a new logo that is more versatile and symbolic

  • Follow the Kik brand

  • Keep it simple

The Result

After countless iterations we settled on a design. It has different color options depending on the context and we wrote a style guide explaining how to use it!
