iOS Share Extension

Kik Messenger

I designed the Kik iOS app extension. Designed to be easy to use and focus on the content being shared. Since I was part of the content team I worked on ways to share content between users, and designing Kik's first share extension was a natural way to do that.

The Goals

The goals of this project were to make sharing content with people and groups on Kik as simple as possible. In addition, to create a UI that could evolve in the future were Kik to add features like sending to multiple people.
