Visual Reminders

The Problem

Huge amount of junk photos build up in the camera roll. Since it's so easy to take photos of things to reference later, camera rolls get full of pictures of posters, slides, and notes. These photos are rarely deleted, take up space, and don't look good next to photos of your vacation.

Capture Temporary Info Easily

Whether you're walking across campus and see a poster for a cool event, or see an important powerpoint slide, sometimes it's easiest to just take a photo. Glance lets you do just that without cluttering your camera roll.
