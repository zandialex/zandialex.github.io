Kik Share Logo

A New Icon for Sharing

The Problem

As we were finishing up the GIF experience and partnering with content providers, we noticed our logo wasn't well suited for environments like an apps share page. The Kik logo would be seen by many people unfamiliar with Kik, and displaying a text logo in such a small space is hard to see.

The Logo

We needed to accommodate for many use cases and backgrounds. I designed a few carefully selected logos, and created a style guide to outline how partners can use them.
