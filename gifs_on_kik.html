GIFs on Kik

Kik Messenger

After Kik acquired the GIF messaging app Relay we needed to add GIFs to the app. I designed the first GIF experience on the Kik app. Through an iterative design process, we killed many ideas to arrive at something that made sense.

The Goals

The goals of this app were to make sharing and discovering GIFs fun and easy. We also needed a way to add curated content.
